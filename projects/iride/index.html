Iride

With this project, I sought the generation of coloured patterns that were simple and at the same time unexpected, unpredictable although partially regular. The algorithm is inspired by an iterative operation of repositioning the pixels and by the linear interpolation between distinct colours.

The Italian term "Iride" has been chosen as the project name for its etymology of Greek origin, which associates it with the term "arcobaleno" (rainbow) and the female name "Iris". The rainbow was personalized in the figure of Iris, the messenger of the gods.
