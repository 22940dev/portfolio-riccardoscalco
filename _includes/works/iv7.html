Date

April 2014

Description

Visual representation of Network Meta Analysis data inspired by atomic orbitals. Direct and undirect comparisons of clinical trials are visualized taking into occount population size, variance and the derived probabilities. The proposal has been presented at The Mario Negri Institute for pharmacological research, in affiliation with the editorial company Zadig.
