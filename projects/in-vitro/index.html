In vitro

Generative artworks based on a summation of Perlin noise functions operating at different scales. The noise has been confined in a circular shape with borders modulated by smoothstep. Colours result from the application of a simple alpha composition of background and foreground colours.

The project has been finalized during the COVID19 outbreak in north Italy, in March 2020. At that time, isolation and confinement were key concepts for the survival of many — separating a vanishing past to a promising future.
